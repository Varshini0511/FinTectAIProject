# NimbusPay Support Knowledge Base

This is the grounding source for the support agent. Answers about policy MUST be
drawn from here — anything not covered should be escalated, not invented.

## Accounts

- A NimbusPay account is opened instantly after identity verification (KYC).
- Standard accounts have a daily transfer limit of **$10,000**. Premium accounts
  have a daily limit of **$50,000**.
- Account statements are generated on the 1st of each month and are available in
  the app under **Documents → Statements** for 7 years.

## Cards

- Each account can have up to **2 virtual cards** and **1 physical card**.
- A lost or stolen card should be **frozen immediately** from the app
  (Cards → Freeze) or by asking support to block it. Blocking is reversible only
  by issuing a new card; a frozen card can be un-frozen.
- Contactless payments are capped at **$100** per tap without a PIN.

## Transfers & Payments

- Domestic transfers settle within **30 minutes**.
- International (SWIFT) transfers take **1–3 business days** and incur a **$15**
  flat fee plus the FX spread (currently ~0.5%).
- A pending transfer can be cancelled from **Activity → Pending** up until it
  settles. Settled transfers cannot be reversed and must be **disputed**.

## Disputes & Fraud

- Customers have **60 days** from the transaction date to file a dispute.
- For suspected fraud, the card is blocked, a provisional credit is issued within
  **10 business days**, and the investigation completes within **45 days**.
- NimbusPay never asks for full card numbers, PINs, or one-time passcodes over
  chat, email, or phone. Any such request is a scam.

## Fees

- No monthly fee on standard accounts. Premium is **$9.99/month**.
- ATM withdrawals: first 3 per month free, then **$2.50** each.
- No fee for receiving domestic transfers.

## Limits & Security

- After 3 failed PIN attempts the card is automatically frozen for 24 hours.
- Two-factor authentication is required for any new payee and for transfers over
  **$2,000**.
